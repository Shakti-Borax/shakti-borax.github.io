Premium Quality Boron Products

Borax, a naturally occurring mineral, is renowned for its multi-purpose applications in industries such as cleaning, glass manufacturing, agriculture, and cosmetics. Our range of boron products, including Borax Decahydrate Crystal, Borax Decahydrate Granular, and Boric Acid Powder, offers high purity and versatility, making them the ideal choice for industrial solutions.

Established in 1991, Shakti Borax Private Limited has grown to become one of the leading Manufacturers and Importers of Boron products in India, and we proudly stand as the largest in North India. Founded by experts with strong backgrounds in Chemical Engineering and Finance, we have built a reputation for excellence and reliability in the Boron trade, earning a distinguished position in the market.
