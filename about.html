Who are we?

Shakti Borax Private Limited manufactures and imports Borates, which are minerals having major constituent as Boron. These minerals are found at rare locations, largest being Turkey and North America. Borates are mined in open pit mines spread across large expanses of land. Widely used commercial borates are Borax Pentahydrate, Borax Decahydrate and Boric Acid. We have been sourcing our raw material from Turkey to produce Borax and Boric Acid since 1994..

Our products have gained widespread acceptance on account of our quality, reliable supply guarantee, customer support and uncompromised association with our distributors. Our manufacturing facility is situated at district Mathura, U.P. within 100 Kms of Delhi, hence well connected to all parts of India.
