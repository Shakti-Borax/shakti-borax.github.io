Who We Are
& What We Do

Shakti Borax Private Limited manufactures and imports Borates — minerals having Boron as their major constituent. These minerals are found at rare locations globally, with Turkey and North America holding the largest reserves.

Widely used commercial borates include Borax Pentahydrate, Borax Decahydrate and Boric Acid. We have been sourcing raw material directly from Turkey to produce Borax and Boric Acid since 1994, ensuring the highest purity and consistent supply.

Our products have gained widespread acceptance on account of quality, reliable supply guarantee, customer support and uncompromised association with our distributors. Our manufacturing facility in Kosi Kalan, Mathura is within 100 km of Delhi — well connected to all parts of India.
